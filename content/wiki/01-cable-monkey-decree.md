# Cable Monkey Decree

## The Cable Monkey Mission Statement

Our mission is to foster a vibrant open source community by encouraging learning, growth, collaboration, and enjoyment. We strive to bring the spirit of our events back to local communities, continuously improving each year to create a more inclusive and engaging environment for all.

## Key Points

- **To Learn** - Embrace new technologies and techniques
- **To Grow** - Develop skills and expertise
- **To Work Together** - Collaborate as a team
- **To Work Safely** - Prioritize safety in all operations
- **To Have Fun** - Enjoy the experience
- **To build the Open Source Community** - Give back to the community
- **To Bring the spirit of the event back to your community** - Share what we learn
- **Always work to improve from one year to the next** - Continuous improvement

*This document establishes the core values and mission for all Cable Monkeys working on the SCaLE Tech team.*
